Biscoff Tiramisu

Surprise your guests with something unexpected: give a Belgian twist to an Italian classic. The magical bond between Lotus Biscoff and coffee adds that little bit extra to this popular dessert. This biscoff tiramisu is a delicious
twist on the classic Italian dessert. It's a dessert that can be both elegant and comforting.

Ingredients:-

  • 3 Eggs
  • 6 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp amaretto
  • 500 g mascarpone
  • 250 g Lotus Biscoff biscuits
  • 1 cup of strong coffee
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder

Method:-

  1. Separate the eggs and beat the yolks and sugar until creamy
  2. Flavour the mixture with the amaretto and add the mascarpone.
  3. Beat the egg whites and carefully fold into the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Place a layer of biscoff on a glass tray.Pour half mixture of mixture. After pouring add another layer of biscoff and pour the rest of mixture.
  5. After pouring rest of the mixture. Use piping bag to decorate tiramisu.
  6. Finish it with sieved cocoa powder.
