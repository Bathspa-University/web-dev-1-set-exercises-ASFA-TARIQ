Turkish Baklava:-

 
            
Servings:-                              36 Servings

            
Cook Time:-                                30 mins

Ingredients:-

    Syrup:-

  • 1 ⅔ cups Water
  • 3 cups Sugar
  • Slice of Lemon

    • Baklava filling:-

  • 1 pound Phyllo pastry sheets
  • Melted Butter
  • 3 Cups Crushed Wallnuts
  • ⅓ Grounded pistachios

Points to be noted:-

*You do not have to use all of the syrup if you feel like
      it is too much or you don't like your baklava too wet.
      *Chop the nuts well.
      *Always cover the phyllo sheet to prevent from breaking
      or drying.

About Turkish Baklava:-

Turkish Baklava, also known as Fistikli Baklava or Pistachio Baklava, is a deliciously rich, buttery, sweet dessert made from phyllo
sheets, finely ground pistachios, butter, and a syrup made from sugar, water and lemon juice.

Direction:-

  1. For making Syrup add water, sugar and a slice of lemon in a saucepan. Bring it to a boil stir continiously untill the sugar dissolves. Reduce the heat and let it simmer for 15 minutes. Then let it cool.
  2. Heat the oven to 350°F and brush a baking dish melted butter.
  3. Trim the phyllo sheets to fit your baking pan. Cover it with a damped cooking towel. To prevent it from drying out or breaking.
  4. Carefully take out one phyllo sheet from the stack and place it on the bottom of the baking tray. Brush the phyllo sheet lightly with melted butter.
  5. Continuing to layer 5-6 more phyllo sheets on top, brushing each sheet with butter until 10 sheets of phyllo have been stacked in
    the bottom of the baking dish. Sprinkle ½ of the crushed Wallnuts over the phyllo base in
    an even layer.
  6. Repeat the process to create another 8-10 sheet layer of phyllo and melted butter, followed by the remaining ½ of the crushed Wallnuts, and another top layer made up of the remaining phyllo sheets and butter. Slice into 36 pieces with a sharp knife in
    square shape, making sure to always cut through all layers of phyllo.
  7. Bake for 60 minutes, until the top is golden brown, and the bottom phyllo layers are baked through. Use a knife to lift up a corner
    of one of the square to see if the bottom layers are done. Check the baklava after 40 minutes and cover with a piece of foil over
    the top if the baklava is getting too browned.
  8. When the baklava is done, remove from oven and slowly pour the cold syrup over the baklava while its still hot. Sprinkle the top
    of each piece of baklava with the remaining finely grounded pistachios.
  9. let it cool in the room temperature.
  10. When it's completely cooled down serve it with turkish cofee without sugar as baklava is already sweet.
